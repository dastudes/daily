            About these Stats
            

                
Stats are pulled live from the official MLB Stats API. Player names link to their Baseball Savant profiles.

                
    
                    
  • RC (Runs Created) = OBP × TB
    • 
                    
  • FIP (Fielding Independent Pitching) = ((13×HR)+(3×(BB+HBP))−(2×K))/IP + 3.10
    • 
                    
  • PAR (Pitching Above Replacement) = (6−(FIP+ERA)/2)×IP/9
    • 
                

                
These are approximations. For the real stuff, see Fangraphs or Baseball Reference.
