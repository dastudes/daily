Daily Brief

The Boston Red Sox and Toronto Blue Jays both gained half a game on the AL East-leading Rays, while the Marlins cut into Atlanta's NL East lead with a win in Colorado. The day's most dramatic finish came at Wrigley Field, where the Cubs edged the Padres in a game that swung 4.29 in WPA.
