Daily Brief

All three division leaders in tight races lost ground on Sunday, with the Braves, Rays, and Dodgers all falling. Atlanta's 10-9 loss to the Mets was among the day's most dramatic finishes, while San Diego and San Francisco both closed a game on Los Angeles in the NL West.
