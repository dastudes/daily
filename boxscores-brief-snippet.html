Daily Brief

The Blue Jays' win at Fenway reshuffled the AL East, with Toronto gaining a full game on the first-place Yankees, who lost at home to the White Sox, while Tampa Bay also picked up half a game. In the NL Central, the first-place Brewers fell to Cleveland in the day's most dramatic game, allowing the Cubs, Reds, and Pirates to each gain half a game.
