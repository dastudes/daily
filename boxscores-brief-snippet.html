Daily Brief

The Pittsburgh Pirates swept a doubleheader from the NL Central-leading Milwaukee Brewers, gaining two games in the division race and tightening a Central where the Cubs and Cardinals also picked up 1.5 games apiece. Milwaukee's lead over the Cubs is now 6.0 games, with four NL Central teams above .500.

  • Pirates 7-6 and 3-2 over the Brewers in a PNC Park doubleheader sweep; Esmerlyn Valdez [3-4, 2 HR | RC 24] led the offense. (PIT)
  • Cubs beat the Reds 5-3 and Cardinals downed the Braves 4-1, with Matthew Liberatore [6 IP, 0 ER, 6 K | 5.02 ERA, PAR 11] tossing six shutout innings. (STL)
  • Phillies defeated the Tigers 4-2 behind seven strong innings from Cristopher Sánchez [7 IP, 2 ER, 7 K | 2.62 ERA, PAR 47], gaining a game on Atlanta in the NL East. (PHI)
  • Diamondbacks routed the Dodgers 9-2, Padres edged the Blue Jays 8-7, and Giants beat the Rockies 4-2 as all three NL West chasers cut into LA's lead; James McCann [2-4, 2 HR] homered twice for Arizona. (AZ)
  • Astros hammered the Rangers 9-3 to close within 2.0 of the AL West lead, with Peter Lambert [6 IP, 1 ER, 7 K | 3.14 ERA, PAR 22] dominant and Yordan Alvarez [2-3, 2 BB, 1 HR | RC 92] homering. (HOU)
  • Rays beat the Mariners 6-1, Yankees topped the Nationals 4-2, Red Sox blanked the Mets 4-0, and Orioles handled the Royals 6-1 behind Kyle Bradish [6.2 IP, 1 ER, 5 K | 3.61 ERA, PAR 26]. (BAL)
  • Twins beat the Angels 5-3, White Sox edged the Athletics 1-0, and Guardians defeated the Marlins 4-1. (ANGELS) (ATHLETICS) (SOX) (GUARDIANS)
  • Ezequiel Duran [2-4, 2 HR | RC 42] hit two home runs in a losing effort for Texas. (TEX)
