Daily Brief

The Chicago Cubs demolished the San Diego Padres 23-3 at Wrigley Field behind Dansby Swanson [3-5, 3 HR | RC 36]'s three-homer day, though the win actually cost them a game in the National wild card race. In the AL Central, the White Sox's grip on first place loosened as Cleveland, Detroit, and Minnesota all won to gain a game on the division leader.

  • Dansby Swanson hit 3 home runs and Michael Conforto [3-4, 1 BB, 2 HR | RC 21] added 2 as the Cubs pounded the Padres 23-3, including an 8-run 8th inning. (CHC)
  • The Tigers stunned the Yankees 6-2 behind Troy Melton [6.1 IP, 0 ER, 7 K | 2.05 ERA, PAR 13]'s scoreless 6.1 innings, in the day's most dramatic game by WPA swing. (DET)
  • The Guardians routed the Rangers 9-4 and the Twins beat the Astros 8-3, both closing ground in the AL Central alongside Detroit. (RANGERS) (TWINS)
  • The Nationals hammered the Red Sox 10-2 at Fenway, with James Wood [2-4, 2 BB, 1 HR | RC 69] homering, gaining a game in the NL wild card race. (WSH)
  • The Rockies edged the Marlins 6-3 and the Giants topped the Diamondbacks 6-4, each gaining a game on the NL West-leading Dodgers, who fell 7-1 to the Athletics. (MARLINS) (DODGERS) (GIANTS)
  • The Brewers beat the Reds 4-2, the Braves handled the Cardinals 5-1, and the Phillies outslugged the Pirates 10-6. (PIRATES) (CARDINALS) (REDS)
  • The Orioles beat the White Sox 6-1 behind Dean Kremer [6 IP, 1 ER, 4 K | 3.18 ERA, PAR 4], the Rays blanked the Royals 4-0 behind Shane McClanahan [6 IP, 0 ER, 4 K | 3.07 ERA, PAR 25], and the Blue Jays beat the Mets 9-3. (BAL) (TB)
